Every site now has an AI summary one click away: hit **TL;DR** in the viewer top bar, or **Summarize** from a site's row menu on the dashboard.

- Summaries are cached — reopening one is instant and free until the site's content changes
- When content changes, the sheet shows what version the summary was written for and offers a one-click update
- Sites with a summary show a sparkle next to their name on the dashboard
